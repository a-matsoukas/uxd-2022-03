Project Proposal

Receipt Tracking for People, centered on Freelancers and Content Creators

What is the problem in brief?


Individuals who are self-employed or work in industries that are not formally recognized have a hard time understanding, tracking, and filing their taxes. Most of the time these individuals are creatives and do not have any tools to understand what tax laws and brackets apply to them. Moreover, they do not take advantage of the specific tax returns or write offs that may be available to them. And even if they are aware of the system, they need to keep track of their daily expenses and understand how to divide personal expenses from business expenses. This is often done right before the tax deadlines, where you submit all the receipts you can get your hands on to a tax consultant and wait for them to figure out what your final tax amount will be. However, many creatives/freelancers who are starting out do not have the financial means to have an inhouse tax consultant. Apart from the logistical issues, there is also a primary behavioral issue where individuals focused on creative work overlook their financial obligations. Building an app catered specifically to this persona will provide them with an easy method to fulfill those responsibilities and, eventually, modify their behavior.

How will your project change the status quo? For whom?


Our goal is that our product can help ease the process of filing taxes for individuals who would otherwise struggle and need to pay an external person to do them. Moreover, it will establish a sense of ownership and responsibility as individuals learn to manage their finances and leverage tax systems. Also, in doing so, we increase our own general knowledge of filing taxes on a personal and a micro scale.

What is (at least) one naïve design idea (rough sketch)? How does this idea address what you see as the core problem?


We think the core problem is not knowing how your expenses and income look like over time, and how that reflects onto your taxes. Our design above aims to fix that with easy to view solutions through graphs and easily digestible boxes of information. However, we decided to not add text to these boxes because we are not part of our users, so we hope to talk to them and figure out what information they would want to be displayed here.

Needs Analysis

Receipt Tracking for People, centered on Freelancers and Content Creators

What problem are you proposing to solve


Individuals who are self-employed or work in industries that are not formally recognized have a hard time understanding, tracking, and filing their taxes. Most of the time these individuals are creatives and do not have any tools to understand what tax laws and brackets apply to them. Moreover, they do not take advantage of the specific tax returns or write offs that may be available to them. And even if they are aware of the system, they need to keep track of their daily expenses and understand how to divide personal expenses from business expenses. This is often done right before the tax deadlines, where you submit all the receipts you can get your hands on to a tax consultant and wait for them to figure out what your final tax amount will be. However, many creatives/freelancers who are starting out do not have the financial means to have an inhouse tax consultant.

Apart from the logistical issues, there is also a primary behavioral issue where individuals focused on creative work overlook their financial obligations. Building an app catered specifically to this persona will provide them with an easy method to fulfill those responsibilities and, eventually, modify their behavior. 

Who matters?


Individuals who are independent creators/freelancers and are at a stage where they do not require or cannot afford external help. They currently do not have any tools that specifically satisfy their unique needs.

How do your prospective users think about this problem/activity?


We think the core problem is not knowing how your expenses and income look like over time, and how that reflects onto your taxes. They are worried about budgeting accurately and making the payments in time. Often, they are people who want to focus on their craft and do not want to deal with the finances of their enterprise. Our design aims to fix that with easy to view solutions through graphs and easily digestible boxes of information.

What requirements must your solution satisfy? (think about different stakeholders)


Our solution needs to solve a handful of critical issues to be viable. Primary of which is the ability to track and classify income. Individuals with multiple income sources or a singular volatile source need the ability to automatically classify their income and consolidate it into small statements month over month. Secondly, a feature that can read income and expense receipts to suggest tax write-offs. And finally, we need to compile all the recorded info into a report that can be directly used to file taxes.

What will the impact of a successful solution be?


A successful solution will help take the stress off of tax reporting day and instill healthy financial behaviors in our users. Moreover, a successful system should also allow our users to focus on what matters to them and focus on building their work.

Narrative:


One of the commonalities we found during our interviews was that stress levels for the people is highly dependent on the type of income that our stakeholder earns. There are several people who fir the Confidence Corey persona and require minimal help as they have established processes or have the means to get help. But on the flip side there are individuals like Bewildered Benny, who does not have a constant flow of income, it is hard to have a set monthly budget and put aside income for taxes. Then there are Future Francis' who recognize that their current methods will only last so long and will require some help to transition from a manual process to a digital one. Our learning was that it is these individuals who have a lack of tools and help from the market. Current software's are catered towards fixed income individuals and only help give general guidelines to those with volatile income sources.

Our goal would be to design a solution that helps educate our stakeholders about the budgeting and tax processes. This will be done dually, first with directly helping them record and keep track of their income and expenses. Secondly, by giving actionable suggestions that are AI driven and make the tax process easier for them. With action and reinforcement we can help our users feel empowered about their finances and feel confident and being in charge of them.
