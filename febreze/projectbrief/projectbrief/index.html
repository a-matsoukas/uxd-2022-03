Project Brief

What is the problem in brief?

The problem we are investigating is the difficulty in finding places that are accessible for people with mobility restrictions. This is especially challenging when in a new area and when things like elevators and automatic doors break.

How will your project change the status quo? For whom?

Status quo: Google, Yelp, TikTok

  • Google gives you too many options and doesn’t filter out anything
  • TikTok is engaging, but hard to search for something specific
  • Yelp is annoying and the reviews aren’t always helpful

Our project will filter out less desirable options, and help the user decide between what is left. This is aimed at people who find themselves to be indecisive, or are traveling in an area that’s new to them.

What is (at least) one naïve design idea (rough sketch)? How does this idea address what you see as the core problem?

An app/service/thing that recommends places to go, things to do, etc within certain locations and categories.

Main functions to include:

  • Explore page

    • Scroll or swipe

  • Search function

    • Dice rolling option to help user randomly choose an activity or place

    • Filtering option based on:

      • Budget
      • Geographical location
      • Activity duration
      • Number of people
      • Etc.

  • Save places to remember

  • Save data for you and your friends on the app to see what people you know like to do

  • Map interface?

  • Add friends & DM

What information do you need to collect? How will you collect it? How will you meet your target users, or who can serve as effective proxies for them?

  • Collect answers by interviewing people
  • Target users are people who have moved to a new area and younger (18-24) people who have traveled to new places and looked for things to do
  • We will meet target users by talking with fellow students and people we know that have moved to new areas
  • Potential interview questions for collecting necessary information:
    • How often do you travel?
    • What kinds of places do you typically travel to, or would like to travel to?
    • What do you like to do when you travel somewhere?
    • Do you prefer local or distant travel destinations?
    • What resources do you wish you had when you traveled somewhere?
    • What is something you would like to improve in your traveling experience?
