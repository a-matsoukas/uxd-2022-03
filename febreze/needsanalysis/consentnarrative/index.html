Consent Narrative

Below is an email template for potential interviewees so we can ensure informed consent.

We are a team of five undergraduate students from Olin College of Engineering. We are currently taking a class called User Experience Design, which focuses on designing interfaces that center usability for groups we are not a part of. Our team is designing an app for people with mobility restrictions and we need to learn more about potential users.

Our goal in this course is to learn how to design for people. Over the course of the semester, we will be creating a more user friendly software solutions for our user group using the insights and observations we learn from our users.

We would love to have a relaxed conversation with the goal of getting to know you as a person and as a member of a ‘people group’. Everything during the course of the interview is optional, and you can end it at any time. Your name and contact information will remain strictly between our team and the teaching staff. We will use pseudonyms to maintain anonymity and omit any possible sensitive information from our reports and presentations.

This course lasts one semester (until mid December) and we hope to meet with you a few times over the course of the project. Interviews will last about 30-45 minutes. We understand that you have other time commitments and will work around your schedule to the best of our ability.
