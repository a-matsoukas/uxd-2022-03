    Design Development What we’ve been working on. Fig.1 - Map with filtered search options and information listed for each location. Our design seeks to make it easier for travelers with physical disabilities to learn about the accessibility of places they want to visit. While the Americans with Disabilities Act seeks to ensure new buildings and spaces are accessible, many existing establishments remain horribly inaccessible to people with certain disabilities. Currently, the primary way people with mobility restrictions figure out if their needs will be met is by calling places.
