    Heuristic Evaluation

    Nov 15, 2022

    As part of our process, another UXD team performed a heuristic evaluation of our user interface prototype we created in Figma. We provided brief instructions to help frame their evaluation. These instructions are below followed by their evaluation.

    Figma Prototype Instructions

    Figma Link

    User Group:

    You are a person with a mobility restriction looking for something to do around Boston. In the case of our prototype, you are looking for somewhere to eat that is accessible to you.

    Key Tasks:

    • Create an account and fill out the introductory quiz questions (you don’t have to put in a name and password)
    • Select The Krusty Krab on the Home page and swipe up to explore the accessibility features and reviews
    • Explore the Map (found on Home page and Search page) and view the search function
    • Look at the profile page and change your accessibility settings (other settings don’t work currently)
